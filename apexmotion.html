About ApexMotion

ApexMotion aims to democratize road safety with scalable, AI-driven ADAS kits tailored for Indian traffic and road conditions. Whether you're a fleet operator, school bus service, or car owner, we provide solutions to monitor, warn, and protect in real time.

Key Features

Collision Warning

AI-based visual systems to detect imminent collisions and alert the driver instantly.

Lane Departure Alerts

Real-time detection of unintentional lane drift with alerts and haptic feedback (optional).

Driver Drowsiness Monitoring

Face and eye-tracking technology to detect signs of fatigue or distraction.

Blind Spot Detection

360° camera and radar inputs for safer lane switching and turns.
